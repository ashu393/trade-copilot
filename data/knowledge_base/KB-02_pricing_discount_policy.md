# KB-02 — Pricing & Discount Approval Policy

Line discounts are governed by an approval matrix:

- 0–10%  — rep discretion, no approval required.
- 11–15% — requires regional manager approval.
- 16–20% — requires director sign-off.
- Above 20% — not permitted on standard orders; requires a contracted price program.

Discounts apply to the order line and are reflected in `discount_pct`. Maximum discount
permitted without director sign-off is therefore 15%.

Promotional pricing is never stacked with Volume Rebate reimbursement on the same SKU in
the same month.
